# Code of Conduct: Code of the Famiglia

## Our Pledge

In the **Famiglia**, we are bound by more than just code; we are bound by respect and collective purpose. We pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community—worthy of the name **"La Passione."**

## Our Standards: Living the "La Passione" Spirit

Members of the Famiglia are expected to uphold the following standards:

* **Respect for the Family**: Demonstrate empathy and kindness toward all members. We are here to build, not to break.
* **Open Dialogue**: Be respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences. A true Consigliere listens before they speak.
* **Constructive Feedback**: Give and gracefully accepting constructive feedback. We grow together or not at all.
* **Accountability**: Taking responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes, and learning from the experience. Integrity is our currency.
* **Focus on the Core**: Focus on what is best not just for us as individuals, but for the overall Famiglia core.

Unacceptable behavior includes:

* **Dishonorable Conduct**: The use of sexualized language or imagery, and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.
* **Hostility**: Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
* **Harassment**: Public or private harassment of any member of the Family.
* **Breach of Trust**: Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or email address, without their explicit permission.
* **Inappropriate Behavior**: Any conduct which could reasonably be considered disrespectful or unprofessional in the context of the Famiglia.

## Governance & The Core

The lead maintainers (The Core) are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior and will take appropriate and fair corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, offensive, or harmful.

The Core has the right and responsibility to remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that are not aligned to this Code of Conduct, and will communicate reasons for moderation decisions when appropriate.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces connected to **"La Passione,"** and also applies when an individual is officially representing the Famiglia in public spaces.

## Resolution Guidelines: The Path of Rectification

The Core will follow these guidelines when addressing violations of our code:

### 1. Correction (The Subtle Warning)

**Impact**: Use of inappropriate language or other behavior deemed unprofessional or unwelcome.
**Consequence**: A private, written communication from the Core, providing clarity around the nature of the violation and an explanation of why the behavior was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.

### 2. Warning (The Firm Word)

**Impact**: A violation through a single incident or series of actions.
**Consequence**: A formal warning with consequences for continued behavior. No interaction with the people involved, including unsolicited interaction with those enforcing the Code of Conduct, for a specified period of time. Violating these terms may lead to a temporary or permanent ban from the Famiglia.

### 3. Temporary Ban (The Quiet Period)

**Impact**: A serious violation of the Famiglia's standards, including sustained harassing behavior.
**Consequence**: A temporary ban from any sort of interaction or public communication with the community for a specified period of time.

### 4. Permanent Ban (The Final Departure)

**Impact**: Demonstrating a pattern of violation of community standards, including sustained harassing behavior, harassment of an individual, or aggression toward or disparagement of classes of individuals.
**Consequence**: A permanent ban from all public and private interaction within the Famiglia.
